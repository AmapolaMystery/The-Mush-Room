Introduction to the blog

Welcome to my blog! (This time, for real!)

I've been wanting to make my own website for a long time. However, I have also been procrastinating for a long time. Thankfully, I gave myself the deadline to make it happen by 2024. Sure, it may be the 30th of December, but hey- I made it happen! huzzah!

The next question is... Whatever will I make of this blog?

I'm gonna be honest, I'm not too sure. I picked up all the code I had already made and published it on impulse. By the time of posting this, the landing page only has placeholder art. The horror!

Oh well. Like I care.

But, hey, this whole writing blog entries thing is coming pretty naturally to me, so who knows- Maybe it won't be that bad.

I just hope this is something I can stick with in the long term, heh...

Although, the more than I think about it, this might actually be feasible. It's not as grandiose or glamorous as my other ideas, so I might just make it. I've even already taken care of the site's main structure! Thanks, past me! I think I'll whip up some quick sketches for the landing page and do some polishing while I'm here. This is already more fun than I thought!

Thanks for coming with me to witness this moderately impressive undertaking, and see you around!

-Amapola Mystery
